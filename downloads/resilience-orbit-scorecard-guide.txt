Resilience Orbit — Scorecard Guide (Quick)

• Availability: Report against SLO (e.g., 99.9%) using error budget terms. If below target, list the top 1–2 contributing incidents.
• MTTR: Use P1/P2 only. If above target, add the bottleneck you will remove next orbit.
• Automation Shipped: Count only safeguards that reduce toil or mean time to recover (kill-switch, retries+jitter, circuit-breaker, failover, probes).
• Game Day: “Pass” means alarm fired, responders paged, service restored under the target (e.g., <5 min), and learning captured.
• Trend: Use arrow ↑/↓ and one sentence: “MTTR ↓ 12 min after automated rollback.”
• Next Orbit: Three priorities that directly close the biggest gap observed.

Share with execs within 48 hours of orbit end.
