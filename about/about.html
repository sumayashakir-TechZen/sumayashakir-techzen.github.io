About Sumaya Shakir

Sumaya Shakir is a technology executive and the creator of Resilience Orbit™, a pragmatic framework for running resilience like a product. She has over two decades of experience leading technology transformations, integrating enterprise platforms, and driving operational excellence for Fortune 100 enterprises and PE-backed companies.

Known for her ability to align technology investments with business priorities, Sumaya has built high-performing global product engineering organizations, enforced PCI and PII compliance, implemented Agile from the ground up, and reduced technology spend by 60% while accelerating execution.

Resilience Orbit™ reflects her belief that resilience should be deliberate, measurable, and sustainable — never an afterthought. Through this framework, she helps organizations embed resilience practices into their culture and daily operations.
